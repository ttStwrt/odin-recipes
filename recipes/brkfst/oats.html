Basic Overnight Oats

HOME

This Overnight Oats recipe makes for a great breakfast on its own, and is a great base for any flavor variation.

Serves 1

Base Ingredients

  • 1/2 c old fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/2 c milk of choice
  • 1/4 plain or vanilla greek yogurt
  • 1-2 tsp maple syrup or honey (other sweetener substitutes okay)
  • Optional: 1 tsp chia seeds
  • Optional: 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Optional: dried fruit, nuts & seeds
Topping Variations
Chocolate PB...
  • 2 tbsp chocolate chips or chunks
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
Berrylicious..
  • 1/4 c fresh berries
  • 1 tbsp jam (such as strawberry chia)

Steps

  1. Combine: Measure out all base ingredients into a lidded container and stir until combined.

  2. Store: Seal jar and place in refrigerator overnight (or at least 2-4hrs).

  3. Serve: Once cooled, remove from fridge and stir to loosen. If mixture is too thick, add milk one teaspoon at a time. (Optional: get creative! Garnish with toppings of choice--see "variations" for topping ideas.)

Adapted from Brittany Mullins' recipe on the eating bird food blog.
