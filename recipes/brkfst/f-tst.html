French Toast

HOME

This is the easiest French Toast Recipe you'll find on the internet, with options to customize and enhance!

Serves 8

Ingredients

  • 8 pieces thick-sliced Brioche bread
  • 1-2 tbsp neutral cooking oil, as needed
  • 1-2 tbsp butter, as needed
For the batter...
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2/3 c milk of choice
  • 1/4 c all purpose flour
  • 1/4 c granulated sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground cinammon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
Optional toppings...
  • Confectioner's (powdered) sugar
  • Fresh fruit or berries
  • Fruit or berry compote
  • Butter to taste
  • Syrup (maple or fruit, such as elderberry)
  • Fresh whipped cream

Steps

  1. Combine: Mix batter ingredients in a shallow bowl either by hand or using a mixer.

  2. Dip: Dip slices into batter mixture, at least dredging on both sides. (Optional: leave dredged pieces of toast in air-tight container overnight to more deeply soak in flavors and speed up prep in the morning.)

  3. Cook: Pre-heat your skillet or griddle to medium-high and grease with a mix of high smoke-point neutral flavored oil (such as avocado) & butter, for flavor. Once skillet is hot, add slices and cook on each side until golden brown, about 3 min.

  4. Serve: Serve immediately with butter and maple syrup. (Optional: get creative! One popular combination is strawberries, powdered sugar and fresh whipped cream.)
